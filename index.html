Worldwide

From a carton to a container, spare parts to steel bars. No matter how large or small a consignment L. B. Freight Ltd will handle it with the same care and consideration.

Confidence

AEOC since 2010, giving customer confidence, dealing with customs matters to a high standard on their behalf. Benefits include:

  • a faster application process for customs simplifications and authorisations
  • a lower risk score which may reduce the number of checks customs carry out on your documents and goods
  • a guarantee waiver up to the level of your deferment account

Banking & Insurance

Not only are your goods taken care of, but L. B. Freight Ltd can organise a full banking service ensuring that you have less to worry about. We are able to negotiate Letters of Credit on your behalf, preparing documentation and dealing with banks to ensure that you are paid promptly. We will fully insure your cargo to the majority of worldwide destinations and can also arrange marine insurance.
